# 1. Website Purpose

This website serves as the official digital headquarters of Atikul Islam Rabbi.

Its primary purpose is to establish professional credibility, showcase expertise, present services, publish educational resources, document case studies, and convert visitors into consultation clients.

The website should never feel like a marketplace profile.

Instead, visitors should feel that they have arrived at the official website of an established consultant.

---

# 2. Website Goals

Primary Goals

• Build trust immediately

• Present a premium personal brand

• Explain services clearly

• Showcase real experience

• Encourage consultation bookings

• Improve visibility in Google Search

• Support AI search engines

• Demonstrate professionalism

• Build long-term authority

---

# Success Metrics

The website should encourage visitors to:

• Learn about Atikul Islam Rabbi

• Understand offered services

• View real case studies

• Watch consultation videos

• Read educational articles

• Contact for consultation

• Visit professional profiles

# 10. Case Studies Page Specification

## Purpose

Explain real-world projects in a structured format.

Educational.

Professional.

Transparent.

---

## Hero

Title

Case Studies

Subtitle

A closer look at selected projects, challenges, and solutions.

---

## Case Study Structure

Every case study follows the same layout.

Overview

Challenge

Analysis

Strategy

Actions Taken

Outcome

Lessons Learned

Recommendations

---

## Challenge

Describe the situation.

Do not expose confidential information.

---

## Analysis

Explain how the problem was evaluated.

---

## Strategy

Describe the planned approach.

Keep explanations professional.

---

## Actions

List completed actions.

Avoid revealing sensitive techniques that should remain private.

---

## Outcome

Describe the result accurately.

If the platform made the final decision, say so clearly.

Never claim guaranteed recoveries.

---

## Lessons Learned

Summarize what readers can learn.

Educational value should always be present.
