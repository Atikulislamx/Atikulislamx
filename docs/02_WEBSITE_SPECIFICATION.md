# 1. Website Purpose

This website serves as the official digital headquarters of Atikul Islam Rabbi.

Its primary purpose is to establish professional credibility, showcase expertise, present services, publish educational resources, document case studies, and convert visitors into consultation clients.

The website should never feel like a marketplace profile.

Instead, visitors should feel that they have arrived at the official website of an established consultant.

---

# 2. Website Goals

Primary Goals

• Build trust immediately

• Present a premium personal brand

• Explain services clearly

• Showcase real experience

• Encourage consultation bookings

• Improve visibility in Google Search

• Support AI search engines

• Demonstrate professionalism

• Build long-term authority

---

# Success Metrics

The website should encourage visitors to:

• Learn about Atikul Islam Rabbi

• Understand offered services

• View real case studies

• Watch consultation videos

• Read educational articles

• Contact for consultation

• Visit professional profiles

# 11. Consultation Page Specification

## Purpose

The Consultation page is designed to convert visitors into qualified clients by offering professional one-on-one guidance.

The page should emphasize clarity, professionalism, and transparency rather than urgency or sales pressure.

Visitors should clearly understand:

• What the consultation includes

• Who it is for

• What to expect

• How the process works

• What happens after the meeting

---

## Hero Section

Title

Book a Professional Consultation

Subtitle

Get personalized guidance for Facebook, Instagram, Meta Business Manager, digital security, and account recovery issues.

Primary CTA

Book Consultation

Secondary CTA

Send Message

Background

Dark matte

Minimal abstract graphics

Professional portrait

---

## Consultation Overview

Explain that consultations are intended to:

• Analyze the client's issue

• Explain possible solutions

• Review evidence

• Recommend the most suitable recovery strategy

• Answer technical questions

Avoid promising successful recovery.

The consultation provides professional guidance based on the available information.
