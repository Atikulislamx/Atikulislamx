# CASE STUDY 01

## Facebook Account Security Consultation

Category

Facebook

Status

Completed

---

### Overview

A client contacted me after losing access to a long-established Facebook account following unexpected login activity.

The objective was to understand the situation, review available information, and recommend an appropriate recovery strategy while improving overall account security.

---

### Challenge

The client experienced:

• Loss of account access

• Login verification issues

• Multiple unfamiliar login notifications

• Recovery uncertainty

---

### Initial Assessment

Reviewed:

• Account history

• Login timeline

• Security settings

• Recovery options

• Platform notifications

---

### Strategy

Prepared a structured recovery plan that included:

• Security review

• Recovery preparation

• Documentation review

• Platform guidance

• Long-term security recommendations

---

### Actions

• Reviewed available information

• Identified possible causes

• Recommended security improvements

• Explained platform recovery options

• Guided the client through appropriate procedures

---

### Outcome

The client successfully completed the recommended recovery process after following the appropriate platform procedures.

Additional security improvements were implemented to reduce future risks.

---

### Lessons Learned

Maintaining updated recovery information and enabling strong authentication significantly improves long-term account protection.
